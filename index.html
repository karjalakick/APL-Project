Services.

Welcome to Rabbit Hole Hotel, we hope to find topics to dive into together with you in endless conversation!

Nestled in Valle di Casies valley, this hotel features a 4.500-m² luxury wellness centre with new Timeless Sky SPA, 8 indoor and outdoor pools with panoramic views of the Dolomites and an outdoor hot tub overlooking the Dolomites. All spacious rooms come with LCD satellite TV and a balcony. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. A 5000-m² garden with a small lake surrounds the Hotel Quelle Nature Spa Resort, which includes 12 different saunas which includes varied outdoor SPA highlights and Kneipp course. Bike hire is free at reception, and ski storage is provided in the winter. All rooms feature wood-panelled walls and a minibar. Soft bathrobes and slippers are provided in each private bathroom. Some rooms overlook the garden, others offer mountain views.
